# Company Handbook

## Email Rules
- Always reply politely
- Never email more than 5 people at once without approval
- Flag emails from unknown senders

## Payment Rules
- Auto-approve recurring payments under $50
- Always ask for approval for payments over $100
- Never pay new/unknown recipients automatically

## Things AI Must NEVER Do Without Approval
- Delete any file or email
- Send money to a new recipient
- Reply to legal or contract emails

## Things AI CAN Do Automatically
- Categorize incoming emails
- Create draft replies
- Log transactions
- Move completed tasks to /Done
- Update Dashboard.md
